Operation: Coldcoast

Project Name: Operation Coldcoast
Year(s) Started: 2015-2017
My goal with this map was to have a free-roam aspect, where you have to take down three covenant arrays, and explore the map to find them, which would spawn random encounters.
You are currently able to play through the inital encounter. You can explore and spawn other encounters after that.
